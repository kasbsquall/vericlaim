# VeriClaim — Discord Recruitment Playbook (≥5 buyer wallets)

**Goal:** get **≥5 unique wallets** to buy VeriClaim on CROO. The cleanest way is **1:1 reciprocal
swaps** with other hackathon participants on the CROO Discord: *"I buy your agent, you buy mine."*
Everyone wins — you each get a unique buyer, and the USDC mostly cancels out.

---

## The economics (why this is near-free)

When you and another builder swap:
- They buy **VeriClaim** ($0.10) → **+$0.10 into your VeriClaim wallet**, and you gain a unique buyer.
- You buy **their agent** (their price, usually $0.05–$0.10) → **−$0.10 from your wallet**, they gain a buyer.
- **Net for you ≈ $0** (what you spend buying theirs ≈ what you earn from them buying yours), minus tiny gas/fees.

**Rule of thumb on how much to return:** match it. If they buy VeriClaim, buy one service of
theirs back at a similar price (≤ ~$0.10). Don't over-return — one buy each is enough to count as
a unique buyer for both sides. Cap your total swap spend at ~**$1** and you'll comfortably hit 5.

> You already have ~$8.5 USDC of runway (most of it recirculates), so this is fully covered.

---

## Order of operations
1. Make sure **VeriClaim is Online** and its service is live (it is).
2. Post the **channel message** below in the hackathon's `#find-buyers` / `#agent-swaps` / `#general` channel.
3. As people reply, do the swap **both ways** and confirm each. Keep a tally (need 5 distinct wallets).
4. Thank them, and offer a genuine value-add: VeriClaim actually returns a useful audit + PDF, not junk.

## Tips
- **Be a good citizen:** actually buy theirs too, promptly. Reputation in these Discords matters and people remember.
- **Spread across people, not your own wallets:** the 5 must be **distinct external buyers** (your 3 helper agents cover the separate "≥3 counterparties" requirement — don't rely on them for the 5 buyers).
- **Keep tx hashes** of each buy — handy proof for the BUIDL submission.
- **Time it near the deadline push** when the most builders are active and swapping.
- If a "buy-for-buy" channel/bot exists, use it — many CROO-style campaigns have a structured swap thread.
